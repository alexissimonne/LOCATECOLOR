L O C A T I O N . C O L O R

MEDIUM: NET ART

4.3.2018

When starting this project I knew that I wanted to incorporate location. After choosing different landscapes, I noticed I chose cold and hot weather locations. I then decided to make this project show warm colors in a warmer landscape setting and doing the same for cooler colors in a cold landscape setting. When you think of the desert, you think of reds and orange toned colors as well as a hot sun. When you see a winter forest, you see the hidden green trees and the blues of the ice/frozen lake. Both the desert and cabin forest are images I vectorized in illustrator and put in as the background image of each page. The hawk is a vector from illustrator and the ice skater is an image from the internet. I found some code to add in the snow which I felt brought that page to life. Hope you enjoy!
